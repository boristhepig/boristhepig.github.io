Stuff We Do

Welfare

We helped 30 pet owners a week with veterinary care and advice and gave out over 3 tonne of animal food to people in need.

Rehoming

We rehomed 600 + animals last year. Dogs like Marvin who had been left in a yard and almost sytarved to death.

Events

We put on a range of events from bingo, carboots to Christmas markets and with your help raised enough money to prevent suffering of another 300 needy animals.

Placements

We work with local schools and colleges to give many students each year the opportunity to do work experience here at Margaret Green Animal Rescue. We also have young people from the Duke of Edinburgh scheme come along and do Gold, Silver and Bronze awards.

Volunteering

As well as placements we have a wide range of volunteer opportunities. Such as Dog walking/socialising, Cat socialising, Grounds maintenance and Retail volunteers
